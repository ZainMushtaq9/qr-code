About QR Code Studio

QR Code Studio is a 100% free online QR code generator designed for everyone — shop owners, students, restaurant owners, freelancers, and personal users. We believe technology should be accessible to all, which is why every feature on our platform is completely free with no signup required.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make QR code generation simple, beautiful, and accessible. We want every small business owner in Pakistan and around the world to be able to create professional QR codes without any technical knowledge or payment.

What We Offer

QR Code Studio provides 12 different types of QR codes, each designed for a specific use case:

  • Image QR Code — Our star feature! Upload your logo or photo and create a branded QR code
  • URL QR Code — Link to any website
  • WiFi QR Code — Let customers connect to your WiFi by scanning
  • WhatsApp QR Code — Instant business chat via WhatsApp
  • vCard QR Code — Digital business card
  • Restaurant Menu QR — Digital menu for restaurants and cafes
  • Payment QR Code — Easypaisa, JazzCash, and UPI payments
  • Location QR Code — Google Maps directions to your business
  • Email QR Code — Pre-filled email composition
  • App Download QR — Link to Play Store or App Store
  • PDF QR Code — Link to any document or file
  • Text QR Code — Encode any message

Our Technology

We use 100% free and open-source technology. No paid APIs, no cloud AI services, no monthly subscriptions. Your data is processed securely on our servers and never stored. Uploaded images are immediately deleted after QR code generation.

Languages

QR Code Studio is available in English and Urdu (اردو). You can switch languages anytime using the toggle in the navigation bar. We believe in making technology accessible in local languages.
