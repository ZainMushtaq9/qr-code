Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At QR Code Studio ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

QR Code Studio is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data (page views, device type, browser) through Google Analytics to improve our services
  • Uploaded Images: Images uploaded for Image QR code generation are processed in memory and immediately deleted after generation. We do not store your images on any server
  • QR Data: The text, URLs, or information you enter to generate QR codes is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
  • Local Storage: Your QR code history is saved locally in your browser using LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Language preference and session management
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense may use cookies to display relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption. Since we don't store personal data, the risk of data breach is minimal.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage tracking
  • Google AdSense — advertisement serving
  • Google Fonts — typography (Inter, Noto Nastaliq Urdu)

Your Rights

You can clear your QR code history anytime from the History page. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. You can opt out of Google's personalized advertising.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at contact@qrcodestudio.com.
